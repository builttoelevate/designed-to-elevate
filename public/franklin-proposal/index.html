The Build

What's included in the build.

A complete, custom-coded website built specifically for Franklin Printing. No templates, no page builders, no monthly website-builder platform fees. Hand-written code that loads quickly and reads cleanly on every device.

The build includes:
  • A full homepage built around your brand, story, and services.
  • Dedicated sections for business printing, mailing services, QSL cards, and any specialty work you want to feature.
  • A custom quote estimator (you can see a working version in the mockup) that lets customers describe their project and request pricing in four simple steps.
  • A "Sample Work" gallery section — built into the site at no additional cost, ready to fill with photos of past work.
  • Mobile-optimized layouts, fast page loads, and clean typography throughout.
