## Cross-cutting patterns (used on every service page)

These are the repeatable persuasion structures the whole site runs on — the most
transferable part for landing pages.

- **Hero formula:** `"[Service], Coshocton Ohio.\nDone right."` — the phrase
  **"Done right."** is the recurring payoff, set in the brand orange.
- **Two-tone headers:** every H2 is a plain phrase + an orange-emphasized kicker,
  e.g. *"Real protection. **Not just a wax.**"* / *"Pick the performance. **Keep the finish.**"*
- **Handwritten "scribble" annotations** reinforce one idea per section:
  `done proper` · `molecular bond, not a topcoat` · `scorpion only` ·
  `carfax on every install` · `half the wash time` · `every drive, cooler` ·
  `pick what you're fixing` · `eric does the install` · `we don't wreck your monday` ·
  `spec to the problem` · `saint-gobain brand` · `nothing we haven't seen`.
- **"Proof, Not Promises" / "Real Results"** eyebrow over testimonials, with the
  headline pattern **"[Customer type]. Zero regrets."**
- **Honest-by-default tone:** straight answers, no overselling. FAQ intros all say
  some version of *"Straight answers… call or text us for anything not covered here."*
- **Price transparency without prices:** a recurring **"What goes into your price"**
  (`PriceFactors`) block lists cost drivers instead of quoting numbers; pricing is
  pushed to the quote wizard / estimate.
- **Deposit/price honesty:** *"The price we quoted is the price you pay; we'll
  always tell you before doing anything that changes it."*
- **Owner-operator proof:** *"Eric does the install himself… no handoff, no
  subcontractors, no rushed crew."* / *"Not a franchise. Not a call center."*
- **Two CTA verbs by service type:** transactional services use **"Get My Quote → 2 Min"**;
  consultative services (residential/commercial) use **"Schedule In-Home Estimate" /
  "Schedule Site Assessment."**
- **Site-wide trust stack:** 15+ years · 52 Google reviews (displayed as "50+") ·
  4.8 rating · appointment-only · certified Scorpion® installer · authorized
  Solar Gard® dealer · System X® certified installer.

- Section headers: *"Three services. **All done properly.**"* / *"Inside & out — **nothing skipped.**"* / *"Every vehicle. **Every situation.**"*

**Key benefits / value props:**
- "Complete job for its scope — no stripped-down versions, no upsell tiers."
- Two-bucket hand wash, microfiber dry (no water spots), paint decontamination
  (iron remover + clay bar), high-gloss sealant, trim restoration.
- "The detail people notice without knowing why" (trim restoration).

**Pain points / customer problems addressed:**

- Heavy-use interiors: "Kids, pets, work, food — daily life is hard on interiors. We've restored vehicles that looked like a lost cause."
- Pre-sale/trade-in value: "A properly detailed vehicle sells faster and for more."

- **Complete Detail** — everything in Interior + Exterior; "showroom-clean inside and out."
- **Add-ons:** Odor Elimination · Pet Hair Removal.

**Trust signals:**
- 15+ years detailing · hand-wash only · appointment-only · 52 Google reviews.
- Honesty: condition-based pricing disclaimer (pet hair, stains, odor, mud, salt, mold may add cost after inspection).

**FAQ / objections handled:**
- How long a detail takes (Interior 2–4h, Exterior 3–5h, Complete 5–8h; most same-day).
- Detail vs. car wash.
- "My interior is really bad — can you still help?" ("that's what we're here for").
- Paint correction is a separate, machine-polishing service (quoted on top).
- Overnight? Most details same-day.
- Deposit: $50 online deposit applied to final invoice; walk-in/phone pay at pickup.
- Trucks & large SUVs (common; pricing may run slightly higher for surface area).

**Persuasive language patterns:**
- "Properly, not quickly." · "Done proper." · "Nothing we haven't seen."
- Who-it's-for grid (Regular Maintenance / Pre-Sale & Trade-In / Heavy-Use / Work Trucks & Fleet).
- Price factors: service scope · vehicle size/seats/rows · condition · add-ons.

## 2. Ceramic Coating

**How it's described (hero / positioning):**
- Eyebrow: *"System X® Certified · Coshocton, OH"*
- Heading: *"Ceramic Coating, Coshocton Ohio. Done right."*
- Sub: *"Professional-grade paint protection using the industry's most trusted
  ceramic. 9H hardness, Boeing-approved chemistry, long-lasting hydrophobic
  performance — backed by one of the strongest warranties in the business."*
- Hero badges: `Hardness 9H` · `Boeing Approved ✓` · `Carfax Registered ✓` · `Warranty Life`
- Section headers: *"Real protection. **Not just a wax.**"* / *"Three tiers of **professional protection.**"* / *"What a coating **actually does for you.**"* / *"The application **process.**"* / *"Easy **to maintain.**"*

**Key benefits / value props (6-up grid):**
- Water Slides Right Off (hydrophobic, self-cleaning) · UV Protection (stops oxidation/fade, holds value) · Deeper Gloss (wet-look, obvious on dark colors) · Chemical Resistance (bird droppings, sap, brake dust, road salt) · Easier to Clean ("most washes take half the time") · Carfax-Documented Value.

**Pain points / problems addressed:**
- Wax/sealant washes off in weeks; coating bonds at molecular level for years.
- "A coating is only as good as the prep" — justifies the process / price.
- Resale value via documented protection.

**Tiers / packages (System X® lineup):**
- **Crystal+™ (Good · Entry) — 2-Year warranty:** high-gloss, super-slick, hydrophobic, UV/contaminant protection, Carfax. "Daily drivers and weekend vehicles."
- **Pro+™ (Better · Most Popular) — 6-Year warranty:** 9H hardness (semi-permanent), ultra-hydrophobic, Boeing approval passed, SGS lab-tested durability, Carfax.
- **Max G+™ (Best · Flagship) — Lifetime warranty:** 30% glossier than prior Max, 4× thicker shell, 2× stronger / 2× more durable, works on paint/wraps/PPF, Carfax.

**Comparison table (Wax/Sealant vs. System X® Ceramic):**
2–3 months → 2 yrs–Lifetime · Washes off → Bonds at molecular level · Mild gloss →
Deep wet-look gloss · No hardness rating → 9H · Reapply monthly → One pro install ·
No warranty → Warranty-backed.

**Process (5 steps):** Thorough Wash → Decontamination (iron remover + clay bar) →
Paint Prep (polish/correct, panel prep) → System X® Applied (panel by panel,
controlled environment) → Cure & Register (12–24h cure, Carfax + System X registration).

**Aftercare (5 steps):** wait 7 days before first wash · pH-neutral soap (warranty
requirement) · no brush-style washes (voids warranty) · microfiber dry · annual
inspections to keep warranty active.

**Trust signals:**
- System X® certified installer · 9H hardness · Boeing-approved chemistry · SGS
  lab-tested (hardness, alkali, salt) · Carfax registration on every install ·
  manufacturer warranty (2-yr / 6-yr / lifetime).

**FAQ / objections handled:**
- Time (most 1–2 full days; prep = as long as the coating).
- "Is it scratch-proof?" — **No** ("anyone who says otherwise is overselling");
  scratch-*resistant*; rock chips need PPF. (Strong honesty/credibility angle.)
- Paint correction first? (locks in swirls if skipped; honest assessment).
- How to wash after coating · can you coat a new vehicle (ideal) · deposit required
  (larger time/material commitment) · how the warranty works.

**Persuasive language patterns:**
- "Real protection. Not just a wax." · "molecular bond, not a topcoat."
- "A coating is only as good as the prep."
- Honest no-oversell on scratch-proofing builds trust.
- Price factors: tier · vehicle size/surface area · whether correction is needed · paint condition.
